Our Story

More Than a Restaurant.
An Experience.

Laadli isn't just about food — it's about creating moments that linger long after the last bite. Nestled in the spiritual city of Mathura, near GLA University, Laadli reimagines the dining experience through a lens of Mughal grandeur and contemporary luxury.

Every arch, every fountain ripple, every golden hue is designed to transport you to a realm where heritage meets modern sophistication. Step in, and you're not just a guest — you're royalty.
